Hi! Welcome to the demo of my radar chart library. I'm Anand Natrajan. I built the library used to see all of the radar charts you'll see in these demos. There are several good radar chart libraries out there, the best being Zalando's open source version. However, I felt I needed a few more features (and didn't need some others), so I built my own.

Before we proceed, a few disclaimers.

  1. I needed some data, so I copied data about different countries from well-known reference sites like Wikipedia, World Population Review, PopulationData.net, World Bank, and of course, Google. Any errors that remain were probably a result of my faulty cut-paste.
  2. I am not trying to make any political statements with this data. Heck, I don't even care if the data is accurate or not (though I tried to pick the most accurate 2021 data). I am trying to demo a charting tool, that's all.
  3. I am not very artistic. Often my choices of colours and fonts are... hideous. Luckily, you can impose your own aesthetic on my library.
  4. Finally, thanks to the many people who gave me constructive feedback, and my employer (Discovery, Inc.) for letting me work on this, and making it open-source.
